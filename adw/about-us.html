About Us

Delivering Results

We have built our Business on quality workmanship, integrity and reputation. This is evident by the amount of repeat business the Company enjoys today signaling our clients confidence with our performance.

In today’s competitive market, we recognize the importance of good customer service and work hard to ensure every client receives personalized attention. This practice forms the basis of a partnership strategy between J Flanagan Welding Solutions and our clients, which promotes excellent communication between both parties.

Areas of Expertise

J Flanagan Welding Solutions is at the forefront of specialist welding techniques in the Northern Territory. We have developed a number of welding procedures that meet the strict compliance of ASME IX and ASME B31.3, AWS D1.1 and AWS D1.6 and relevant United Kingdom codes/standards for piping and structural.

J Flanagan Welding Solutions are very experienced in all types of piping fabrications from basic straight spools to highly complex configurations for low pressure systems to extremely high pressure applications.

Our combination of operation/management systems along with practical hands on experience along with our flexible and loyal workforce is another key to our continued success, providing us with a real advantage.

Some of the work carried out by J Flanagan Welding Solutions include but are not limited to the following:

  • Specialised Welding of Exotic Pipe Spooling
  • Duplex Flow Lines
  • High Pressure Piping
  • Process Piping
  • Cu Ni Piping
  • Cr Mo Piping
  • All Carbon Steels
  • Austenitic/Duplex Stainless Steels
  • Oil and Gas Piping Systems
  • On & Offshore Work Teams
  • Flow Lines & Test Equipment Spooling

Weld Procedures

We have approved weld procedures in place for MMAW, GTAW, FCAW and GMAW (STT). These procedures are both in plate and pipe in all sizes.

Materials covered by these procedures include all Carbon Steels, Chrome Moly Alloys, Austenitic Stainless Steels, Duplex Stainless Steels, Aluminum Brass, Copper and Copper Nickel.
