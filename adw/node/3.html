Our Specialist Team

ADW Welding’s Managing Director, Grant Ryan, has 20 years of post-trade experience in the manufacture and welding of various piping systems working both
on and offshore as a Welder and Welding Supervisor. A Boilermaker by trade, Grant also has International Welding Practitioner (IWP), International Welding Specialist
(IWS), International Welding Inspector (IWIB) and Certified Welding Inspector (CWI) qualifications and is the company’s Responsible Welding Coordinator (RWC).

Tracy Ryan, the Administration Manager, is responsible for ensuring compliance across all aspects of the business. Tracy has extensive Health, Safety & Environment
experience and an Advanced Diploma in Occupational Health & Safety.

The company currently employs thirteen qualified tradesmen and has access to a pool of other welding professionals as and when required.

Professional development is credited as a major force within the Company, with each new staff member carefully inducted into the stringent controls of the workplace environment. Each member of the ADW Welding workforce is trained and qualified to perform welding to relevant welding procedures.

Further specialised training for all offshore personnel such Tropical Basic Offshore Safety Inductions and Emergency Training (TBOSIET), Working at Heights, Confined Space and Fire Fighting Training is undertaken.

All offshore personnel obtain the Maritime Security Identification Card (MSIC) and Common Safety Training Program (CSTP) as well as holding a current Oil and Gas UK Medical Examination Certificate (OGUK).
